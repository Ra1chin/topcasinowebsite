Blackjack Strategies That Work

Blackjack is a game of skill and strategy, and knowing the right moves can help you reduce the house edge and increase your chances of winning. Here are a few key strategies to get you started:


  • Basic Strategy: Always follow a chart based on your hand and the dealer’s upcard. This minimizes the house edge and guides your decisions on whether to hit, stand, double down, or split.

  • Card Counting: This advanced technique involves keeping track of the high and low cards remaining in the deck. By adjusting your bet size based on the count, you can improve your odds.

  • Avoid Insurance Bets: While tempting, insurance bets generally have a higher house edge, so it’s better to skip them.

  • Manage Your Bankroll: Set limits for your bets to ensure you play within your budget, reducing the risk of large losses.
